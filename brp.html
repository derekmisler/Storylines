Vicki’s Top Tips for Snapping Great Parkway Pics

Take advantage of sweet light hours in the morning and in the late afternoon. Embrace different kinds of weather—fog and mist can be your friends in the mountains.

Take horizontal and vertical shots of the same image.

Use the “rule of thirds” method for shooting. Don’t place your subject in the center of every shot; move it around to the different corners of the frame.

Wear decent shoes. Hiking boots are the best.

Be patient, take your time, and keep your eyes and mind wide open; there’s beauty all around you. Check out the overlooks, hike the trails, breath in the fresh mountain air and capture the moments.
