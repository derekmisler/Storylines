• From the Asheville Visitors Center, get on I-240 W.
  • Keep right to continue on 1-26 E, and follow this road for 22 miles to Exit 49A.
  • Merge onto Hwy 64 E and follow this road for 13 miles.
  • Turn right onto NC 9 / US 74 Alt, and go 2.5 miles to the entrance of Chimney Rock State Park.
  • From Chimney Rock, turk left onto NC 9 / US 74 Alt, and remain on this road for 20 miles.
  • Continue on I-240 W and take Exit 4C, following the signs back to the Asheville Visitors Center.

Travel Time: 1 hour 30 min.
